Our Story

Family-run Vietnamese eatery bringing authentic bánh mì to Templestowe Lower. Fresh baguettes baked daily, house-made pâté, pickled vegetables, and premium fillings. A taste of Saigon in the suburbs of Melbourne.

Fresh Daily.

From crusty baguettes to vibrant herbs, every roll is made to order with that unmistakable street-food punch.
